Privacy Policy

DownhillTracker respects your privacy. This page explains what data the app uses and why.

Location Data

The app uses your device location to:

  • Display your position on the map
  • Record your session track when tracking is active
  • Calculate speed, distance, elevation gain/drop

If background tracking is enabled, the app may collect location while the screen is off to continue recording a session.

Weather (Optional)

If enabled/available, the app may fetch temperature for your location to store with a saved session. This requires an internet connection.

Data Storage

Saved sessions (including track points and summaries) are stored locally on your device. The app does not upload your session history unless you explicitly share it.
