Privacy Policy

DownhillTracker respects your privacy. Here’s what the app uses and why.

Location Data

The app uses location to:

  • Show your position on the map
  • Record tracks during a session
  • Compute speed, distance, and elevation stats

If background tracking is enabled, the app may continue collecting location while the screen is off to keep recording a session.

Weather (Optional)

The app may fetch temperature near your location to store with a saved session. This requires internet.

Data Storage

Session history is stored locally on your device. The app does not upload your sessions unless you explicitly share them.
