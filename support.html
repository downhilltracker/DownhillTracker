FAQ

Why is my location/city incorrect?

GPS gives coordinates; the city label comes from reverse geocoding and can be wrong near borders or when accuracy is low. Check your GPS accuracy in the Track screen.

Why does the map not center on launch?

Location permission may be off, or your device hasn’t provided a recent fix yet. Try opening Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

How do I enable background tracking?

Turn on “Background tracking” in Settings. You may also need to grant “Always” location permission.

How do I use Demo Mode?

Enable Demo Mode in Settings and choose a resort. The app will generate a simulated track for testing.
