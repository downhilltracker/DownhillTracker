Common questions

Why is my location label wrong?

GPS provides coordinates; place labels come from reverse geocoding and can be inaccurate near borders or when accuracy is low. Check the reported accuracy in the app.

How do I enable background tracking?

Turn on “Background tracking” in the app’s Settings and ensure location permission allows tracking.

How does demo mode work?

Demo mode generates a simulated track around a selected resort for testing and screenshots.
